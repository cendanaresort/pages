Cendana Resort & Spa Ubud

Overlooking the beautiful rice fields of Bali, Cendana Resort & Spa provides air-conditioned rooms with traditional Balinese d√©cor and free Wi-Fi. Pampering spa treatments, 2 outdoor pools and free parking are available.

The spacious rooms are fitted with a four-poster wooden bed and opens out to a private balcony. A TV, minibar and mosquito nets are provided. The en suite bathroom has a bathtub and shower.

Cendana Resort & Spa is a short 5-minute walk from the famous Monkey Forest Ubud. It is within a 15-minute walk from Ubud Centre and Puri Lukisan Museum. Ngurah Rai International Airport is an hour's drive away.

Airport transfers and packed lunches are available at an extra charge. The resort also provides laundry services and a 24-hour front desk.

Sun Rise Restaurant serves Indonesian and international cuisines, as well as a daily buffet breakfast. In-room dining is possible with room service.
